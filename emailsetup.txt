Quick EmailJS Setup (5 minutes):
Step 1: Create Account
Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
Click "Sign Up" (it's FREE)
Use your email: srushtichauhan2006@gmail.com
Create a password and verify your email
Step 2: Add Email Service
After logging in, click "Email Services" in the left menu
Click "Add New Service"
Select "Gmail"
Click "Connect Account" and sign in with your Gmail
You'll get a Service ID (like service_abc123) - COPY THIS
Step 3: Create Email Template
Click "Email Templates" in the left menu
Click "Create New Template"
Give it a name: "Booking Confirmation"
In the template editor:
Subject: Booking Confirmation - {{car_name}}
Content: Click "Edit" and paste this:
Dear {{to_name}},

Your payment of {{booking_amount}} for {{car_name}} has been confirmed!

Transaction ID: {{transaction_id}}
Payment Method: {{payment_method}}
Booking Date: {{booking_date}}
Inspection Date: {{inspection_date}}

Your Details:
Phone: {{buyer_phone}}
Address: {{buyer_address}}

Need help? Call {{support_phone}}

Thank you for choosing RideBazzar!
5.Click "Save" and you'll get a Template ID (like template_xyz789) - COPY THIS
Step 4: Get Public Key
Click "Account" in the left menu
Go to "API Keys" tab
Copy your Public Key (like abcXYZ123)
Step 5: Update Your Code
Now open your payment.html file and make these changes:

Change 1 (around line 11):
emailjs.init({
  publicKey: "PASTE_YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE", // Replace with actual key
});
Change 2 (around line 567):
emailjs.send('PASTE_SERVICE_ID_HERE', 'PASTE_TEMPLATE_ID_HERE', templateParams)
Example:
If your keys are:

Public Key: abcXYZ123
Service ID: service_gmail
Template ID: template_booking
Then update like this:
emailjs.init({ publicKey: "abcXYZ123" });
emailjs.send('service_gmail', 'template_booking', templateParams)
That's it! After this, every booking will send a real email to the customer's inbox.
