Miscellaneous charges and fees

Additional fees that may apply during the used car loan process, covering various administrative and service-related costs. Some of these charges are listed below:

  • Credit Report Charges: Some lenders might charge a fee to obtain your credit report, which is used to assess your creditworthiness.
  • Change in Loan Terms: Making changes to the loan terms, such as the tenure or interest rate, may incur administrative charges.
  • EMI Bounce Charges: In case your Equated Monthly Instalment (EMI) payment bounces due to insufficient funds, you may incur a fee.
  • Loan Agreement Charges: The preparation and notarization of the loan agreement can involve fees.
  • Overdue EMI Charges: If you miss an EMI payment, there may be a penalty charge applied for each day the payment is overdue.
  • Late payment fees: EMI payments beyond the grace period may result in late payment fees.
  • Foreclosure Charges: If you decide to repay your loan before the agreed-upon tenure, you might face foreclosure charges. These charges are typically a percentage of the outstanding loan amount.

It's crucial to thoroughly review the terms and conditions to understand any additional charges that may apply. With this clear breakdown of all charges associated with your used car finance, we reiterate our commitment to transparency and building trust.
